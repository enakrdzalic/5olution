About 5pectrum

Inspiration: There is a growing need for securing our personal belongings. We want to make an security app that alerts the user whenever their personal belongings have been removed from original location.

Target Users: Our target users are individuals who wish to keep track of the use and location of their belongings. Use cases can include: limiting the use of cellphones, prevent losing car keys, and notifying the user when items are taken unknowingly.

How It Works: The Raspberry Pi color sensor detects the colour of the object and assigns it an RGB value. Items are registered according to their colour and displayed on the web app along with the user's log history.
