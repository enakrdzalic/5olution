About 5pectrum

Inspiration: There is a growing need for proper sleep with technology booming and addictions forming. We want to make a health-aid that encourages users to put down their gadgets and live a healthier life. We've all been guilty of playing with our smartphones for an extra hour instead of sleeping. Register your item using a color-tag, set a time range, and give your body a well-deserved break from technology. Our system will alert you if your item is removed before the designated end time so you'll remember when to keep your gadgets down and enjoy some proper me-time.

Target Users: Our target users are individuals who need a little encouragement in putting down their devices and getting back to a healthy lifestyle. Use cases can include: limiting the use of cellphones, encouraging walking short distances instead of driving, and monitoring time children spend on electronics. Discover an infinite spectrum of possibilities by putting your distractions behind.

How It Works: The Raspberry Pi color sensor scans your object, detects a colour off the spectrum, and assigns it an RGB value. Items are then registered according by colour and displayed to the web app, where the user can set the start and end time which they would like to keep that item down. An alarm will sound if the item is removed before the designated end time.
